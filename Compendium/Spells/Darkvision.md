### *2nd-level Transmutation Spell*

**Casting Time:** 1 Action

**Range:** Touch

**Components:** V, S, M

**Duration:** 8 Hours

You touch a willing creature to grant it the ability to see in the dark. For the duration, that creature has darkvision out to a range of 60 feet.
